Budget Cycle Timing

Most hospital fiscal years start October 1 or January 1. Capital planning happens 2–3 months before close. If you are not in the budget conversation by Q2, you are waiting another year. Get in front of the right people now — even for a next-year deal.

Regulatory Pressure

CMS and Joint Commission are increasing supply chain visibility and equipment maintenance requirements. Manual logs and paper-based counts are no longer defensible at audit. Hospitals automating now arrive at audits with real-time data — not a spreadsheet from last quarter.

Nursing Shortage & Burnout

The nursing shortage is structural and worsening. Hospitals cannot afford their remaining nurses spending 2+ hours per shift on logistics. Every efficiency gain is a retention tool. Kontakt.io is one of the few investments that pays nurses back in time from day one — and your CNO will connect that to turnover cost immediately.

AI Mandate from Leadership

CFOs and COOs are being asked by hospital boards to demonstrate AI ROI. SCA is one of the clearest, most measurable answers available: AI-driven supply routing with a quantifiable 30% cost reduction. Give your champion a story they can take upstairs with a number attached to it.

Competitive Urgency — Use This

Example talk track: "Large health systems are accelerating RTLS rollouts system-wide right now. If you are in a competitive market, your CNO will be asked about your operational technology roadmap by the board. The hospitals that deploy first set the benchmark — and the gap compounds."

Post-Pandemic Supply Chain Mandate

COVID exposed catastrophic supply chain fragility in hospitals. Health systems are now under board mandate to build resilient, visible supply chains. SCA directly answers that mandate — and in many systems, there is dedicated budget attached to supply chain modernization that did not exist before 2020.

Renewal Window (90 Days Out)

90 days before renewal is the optimal moment to show SCA value and plant the expansion seed. Too early and it feels like a sales pitch. Too late and procurement is already in evaluation mode. Trigger the expansion conversation in your next QBR if you are in that window.

New Hospital or Wing Opening

A new facility opening is the single best expansion trigger in the portfolio. Infrastructure decisions are being made in real time. Get in front of facilities and IT during the planning phase — it is the lowest-friction, highest-impact moment to add SCA to the scope.

New Executive Sponsor

A new CNO, VP Supply Chain, or CIO is an executive reset. They want to put their mark on operational decisions. Kontakt.io — especially an SCA expansion — is a forward-thinking initiative they can claim. Position your conversation as giving them an early win to bring to their leadership team.

Upcoming Joint Commission Audit

JC audits create urgency around documentation and compliance that resonates with CNOs and VPs of Quality. The AT platform's PM tracking and SCA's supply visibility are both directly relevant to JC requirements. Time the SCA expansion conversation to land during audit prep — the value prop is self-evident in that context.
